Daiquiri

Description

A nice, crisp daiquiri is one of the best pleasures a human being can feel. Something about the blend of rum, lime juice and a little sugar just blends together harmoniously

It is also very versatile thanks to the many styles of rum. It can range from clean and crisp to funky from jamaican rum to barreled age almost like whiskey.

Ingredients

  • 2oz rum
  • 1oz lime juice
  • .75oz simple syrup
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Make simple syrup, weigh out a cup of sugar and combine with a cup of hot water. Stir to dissolve the sugar and let cool.
  2. Add rum, lime juice and simple syrup to a cocktail shaker. Add a handful of ice and close the lid.
  3. Shake vigorously for 15 seconds, double strain into a chilled couple
  4. Garnish with a fresh or dehydrated lime wheel
  5. Enjoy 1 or 2 more!
