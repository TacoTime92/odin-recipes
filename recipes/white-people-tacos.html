White People Tacos

Description

Feel lazy but want something tasty? Well look no further than white people tacos!

Where the most diligent task is browning ground beef this recipe will have you eating good with minimal effort. Just watch out for the spice Karen!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Shredded Cheese, mexican blend
  • Tortillas, flour, corn, or hard shells make no difference
  • Salsa, if filling spicy
  • Lettuce, shredded

Steps

  1. On the stove, heat a pan over medium-high heat, add ground beef and brown the meat, drain the excess fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water to the browned beef, and let simmer for 5 minutes
  3. If hard shell tortillas, place in oven to warm up. If soft shell tortillas place in microwave with a damp towel for 20 seconds
  4. Once tortillas and ground beef, assemble your tacos.
  5. I like to add cheese first, then beef, then more cheese, then salsa, then lettuce if I'm feeling heathy!
  6. Enjoy your white people tacos!
