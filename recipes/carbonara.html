Authentic Carbonara

Description

This authentic tasting carbonara dish is incredibly easy to make and will leave you and your loved ones drooling. All while using a total of 6 ingredients.

Authentic carbaonara uses no cream, as the creaminess comes from eggs, cheese and the pasta water. This quick recipe will have you and your guests eating good within 40 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 5 Eggs
  • Fresh shredded cheese (Parmesan or Romano preferred), little over a cups worth
  • 1/2lb Guanciale (Pancetta or Bacon work as well)
  • 1lb Pasta
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan on the stove, once hot throw on diced guanciale (or pancetta/bacon) and start rendering
  2. In a bowl separate 3 egg yolks and add two whole eggs, whisk and add cheese to combine a slurry of sorts
  3. Bring a pot of water to boil, once boiling add the pasta
  4. Once pasta is done cooking add to pan and turn off heat
  5. Add some pasta water to the cheese slurry to temper and slowly add to the pan, stirring to combine with Pasta
  6. Once the entire slurry is combine with pasta, add salt and pepper to taste
  7. Plate, add some extra cheese and pepper on top and enjoy!
